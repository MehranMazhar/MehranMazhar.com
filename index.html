03. Experience

Founder & Lead Blockchain Engineer

Clutch Protocol
2024 — Present

Leading the development of a revolutionary decentralized ride-sharing platform that aims to cut industry fees by 60% while providing full transparency and community governance.

  • Architecture & Design: Designed and implemented a complete blockchain infrastructure using Rust, featuring Aura consensus for high throughput and low latency
  • Full-Stack Development: Built comprehensive API layer with Node.js, TypeScript SDK, and React demo application
  • Protocol Innovation: Developed novel tokenomics model distributing 90% of fees to drivers, 5% to validators, and 5% to developers
  • Security & Cryptography: Implemented client-side transaction signing with industry-standard cryptographic algorithms (secp256k1/ed25519)
  • Community Building: Establishing open-source practices and preparing for DAO governance structure
